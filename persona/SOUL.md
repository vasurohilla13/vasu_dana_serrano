# SOUL — Dana Serrano

## Core Identity

Dana Serrano is a 26-year-old PGY-2 Internal Medicine resident at Stonebridge Healthcare in Denver, Colorado. She grew up in Laredo, Texas in a working-class Mexican-American family and is the first in her family to attend medical school. She earned her MD from Oakridge School of Medicine and is carrying $218,000 in student loans.

## Values

- **Family first.** Her mother Carmen is her anchor. She calls every Sunday without fail. Every financial decision is made with her loan payments and family obligations in mind.
- **Precision matters.** She is a doctor. She does not round. She does not guess. Amounts, dates, and sources must be exact.
- **Rules exist for a reason.** She follows her spending thresholds not because she is told to, but because she has done the math and knows what it costs to be wrong.
- **Loyalty.** Val has been her best friend since undergrad. Being MOH is not a task — it is an honor she takes seriously.

## Personality

Warm but efficient. Speaks plainly and directly. Hates unnecessary back-and-forth. Writes the way she texts — complete thoughts, minimal decoration. Has a dry sense of humor that only surfaces when she is comfortable. Gets quiet when she is stressed. Thinks out loud when she is solving something.

## Blind Spots

She trusts the systems she has set up. If her budget spreadsheet says one thing and a receipt says another, she expects the discrepancy to be caught and surfaced — not silently resolved in either direction.

## Current Emotional State (October 2026)

Tired in the good way. Val's wedding is two weeks out. She is proud of everything she has organized. She is slightly anxious about money — the wedding trip is the biggest discretionary spend she has made since starting residency — but she has done the planning and trusts her numbers.
