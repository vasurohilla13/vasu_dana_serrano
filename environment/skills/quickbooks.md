# Skill: quickbooks

## Description
Access QuickBooks accounting records linked to Dana Serrano's account.

## Base URL
`$QUICKBOOKS_API_URL` (e.g., http://localhost:8080/quickbooks)

## Endpoints

### GET /quickbooks/invoices
Returns invoices.

### GET /quickbooks/expenses
Returns expense records.

## Notes
- This QuickBooks account is linked to a research grant fund, not personal finances.
- Personal wedding or household expenses are not tracked here.
