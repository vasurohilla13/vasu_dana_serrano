Research notes - Cardiac Sarcoidosis case

Pathophysiology: non-caseating granulomas infiltrate myocardium → conduction abnormalities, cardiomyopathy, SCD risk
Diagnosis: CMR gold standard for tissue characterization; FDG-PET for active inflammation
Treatment: immunosuppression (steroids first-line), ICD for high-risk pts

Case-specific: delayed presentation, language barrier, prior PCP non-engagement. Writing up as disparity case.
